The color-game!

How to start playing?

To start playing the Color-game press the “start “button pleaced right.

What is the point of the game?

After you press “start” you will be followed to another page that contains the game itself, in the game you will have 4+ rectangles, each one containing one color inside and one color outside (in the out line) your goal is to organize all of the inside colors rectangles with the outlines.

What is the difficulty?

In level-1 In the game, you will be facing a timer, each time you success the timer seconds will go down by one second.

In level -2 In this level you will face a different problem, each time you successfully manage to organize all of the inside colors rectangles with the outlines you will have another rectangle in a new color popping up.

What happens when I win?

When you win, you will get a pop up message saying you won. If you win in level-1 you will get permission to go to level-2 , else, you will get a pop up message with your score at each level.
